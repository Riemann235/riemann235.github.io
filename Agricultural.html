ADT Grant – Ardoch Agricultural Society

The funding provided by the Ardoch Development Trust was used to purchase new section & safety signage and additional safety barriers/posts. These improvements were very successful in assisting the general public navigate around the Show, including the introduction of a new car park.This was a major success and the intention is to follow the same arrangements in 2020 as we used in 2019.

In terms of the new safety barriers/posts etc, these proved to be much more effective than in previous years. The Committee are considering a redesign of the Show ring and this may lead to the need to purchase some additional barriers. However, the Committee of Ardoch Agricultural Society would like to express its sincere thanks to the Ardoch Development Trust for its generous support.
