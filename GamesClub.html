ADT Grant – Braco School Games Club

Braco Parent Council are grateful to the Ardoch Development Trust for supporting our after school Braco Games Club. The club has proved very successful, being fully subscribed for both the Winter and Spring terms this year. In addition to the 16 children signed up each week, we have also welcomed many other children with adult supervision. Braco Games Club has proved a great opportunity for children to play together outside of school and provided parents with a much valued childcare opportunity. As well as many traditional board games, the funding provided by ADT has allowed us to source new and innovative games that have inspired the children’s enthusiasm. For example, we have been able to source some co-operative games that have the children working as a team to ‘beat the game’. ADT funding has been particularly helpful for Games Club to have a range of games that cater to all abilities. Braco Games Club will continue to run until at least the Easter holiday’s.
