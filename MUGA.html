ADT Grant – Multi User Games Area

Wednesday 16th December 2020 was a special day for our community. After more than three years of discussions, grant applications, tendering and the small matter of a global pandemic to hold up proceedings, the MUGA (multi-use games area) was finally completed and handed over to Ardoch Development Trust by our contractors, Doesport.

Four sports - tennis, basketball, netball and football - can now be played on the site behind Braco Village Hall, safely surrounded by a splendid green fence to protect those using the nearby play area. The Trust now hopes to encourage the community to form a Sports Club to manage and maintain the MUGA and allow locals of all ages to make use of this wonderful new facility.

Fiona Watson - Ardoch Development Trust
