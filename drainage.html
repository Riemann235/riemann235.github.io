ADT Grant – Braco Lodge Park drainage

Braco Show has been held at Lodge Park in Braco for many years. The park is a recreation ground for the village and own by Mr N Muir who gives us kind permission to hold the show there every year. There has been a low lying area of the field which is often wet and after the show days foot fall and movement in and out of trade stands it does get quite muddy. Last year the Shows committee decided to look into putting a drain in this part of the field to help us out and give something back to the park and all those who use it. This work was carried out in May 2018 by MAM Contractors who are one of 4 Main Sponsors of Braco Show. With the dry summer we had the drain hasn’t been fully tested yet but the down pour on the day did drain away very quickly allowing everyone to continue enjoying their day. Hopefully it will go on to be a worthwhile investment for years.
